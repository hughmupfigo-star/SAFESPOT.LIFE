Food Is
Medicine

Nutrition, Healing & The Alkaline Body. What you eat is a conversation with your body. Learn to speak the language of healing. This course breaks down how food became an industry, what your body actually needs, and how to reclaim your health through alkaline living, fasting, and conscious nutrition.

What You'll Learn

  • Module 1: You Are What You Eat — The foundational truth that every healing tradition already knew. What food does to your cells, your hormones, your mind — and why the industry needs you not to know.
  • Module 2: God-Made vs Man-Made — The difference between food that builds and food that destroys. How to tell them apart, and why processed food was engineered for addiction, not nutrition.
  • Module 3: The Alkaline Body — What pH balance actually means for your health. How modern food creates an acidic environment where disease thrives — and how to shift it back.
  • Module 4: The Power of Fasting — Why hunger is one of the body's greatest healers. Different fasting approaches, what happens during a fast, and how to use it safely and intentionally.
  • Module 5: What Junk Food Does to You — The full picture of what ultra-processed food does to your gut, brain, hormones, and energy — and why it's designed to keep you coming back.
  • Module 6: Healing Foods and Herbs — Nature's pharmacy. Which foods actively heal and why, which herbs address specific conditions, and how to integrate them into everyday life.
  • Module 7: Children and Family Nutrition — How to protect your children from a food system designed to hook them early. Building healthy habits for the whole family without turning food into conflict.
  • Module 8: Detoxing and Transition — How to move away from harmful food patterns without overwhelm. What detoxing actually means, what symptoms to expect, and how to support your body through change.
  • Module 9: Building Your Alkaline Life — Putting it all together. A practical, sustainable approach to food as a daily healing practice — not a diet, a way of living.

Healing, Not Restriction

This isn't about another diet. It's about understanding what food was before the industry got hold of it, and reclaiming the relationship your body was designed to have with the earth.

Food is not a reward, a punishment, or a product. It is information. This course teaches you to read it.
