Our Mission: No young person should apologise for existing.

SAFE is building holistic youth development hubs for at-risk young people. Every role in this organisation exists to serve that mission—to build a place where young people can show up fully as themselves, develop real skills, and find genuine community.

Operations Manager

You'll be the person who keeps the trains running. Finance, admin, HR stuff, vendor management, logistics for the studio equipment. You'll build systems and processes that let the rest of the team focus on the mission. You'll anticipate problems before they happen. You'll make chaos into order.

Location: UK (hybrid) · Type: Full-time

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage finance, budgeting, and accounting
  • Handle HR, recruitment, and people operations
  • Manage vendor relationships and procurement
  • Coordinate logistics for studio equipment
  • Build and maintain operational systems and processes
  • Anticipate and solve problems before they impact mission

You'll Learn

  • Running operations at a high-impact social enterprise
  • Cross-functional systems thinking
  • Building operational resilience and efficiency
  • Non-profit finance and governance
  • How good ops enables mission delivery
  • Managing complexity in resource-constrained environments

We're Looking For

  • 5+ years in operations or business administration
  • Strong finance and accounting knowledge
  • Experience with HR and recruitment
  • Excellent systems thinking and problem-solving
  • Comfortable with ambiguity and early-stage organisations
  • Commitment to using operations to enable mission impact
