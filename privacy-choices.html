Your Privacy Choices

Managing Your Information

We believe you should have control over your personal data. Below are the choices available to you regarding how SAFE collects, uses, and shares your information.

Communications & Marketing
You can opt out of receiving marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email communication or by contacting us directly at enquiries@safespot.life.
Data Access & Deletion
You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at enquiries@safespot.life with your request and verification information.
Payment Information
Payment information is processed securely through third-party payment providers. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers. Your payment information is subject to the privacy policies of our payment processors.
Analytics & Tracking
This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics services that profile your behavior. We collect minimal usage data only to maintain and improve the site itself.
Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share information when necessary to complete a transaction or as required by law.

Do Not Track

If your browser supports the "Do Not Track" signal, your preference will be respected. This website does not track your online activities across different websites.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under data protection laws such as GDPR. For more information about your rights or to exercise them, contact us at enquiries@safespot.life.

Contact Us

Questions about your privacy choices? Contact: enquiries@safespot.life
