Youth Stories — What Matters

What's happening
in your world that
no one's talking about?

Mainstream media misses the real stories. The ones happening on your streets. In your schools. In your communities. We want to hear directly from you about what's actually going on—the good, the bad, the complicated. Your story matters.

Why we're listening

You know what's really happening. The press often doesn't.

Your perspective matters

You're living it. You see the patterns. You understand the stakes. Media outlets chase clicks—they miss the actual reality of what young people are dealing with every day.

It doesn't have to be positive

We're not looking for feel-good stories. We want the truth. The struggles. The systemic failures. The gaps in what's being covered. The darkness alongside the light.

Connection is the antidote

When you share your story, other young people feel less alone. They realize they're not crazy for seeing what's broken. Community gets built when we tell the truth about what we're facing.

This is a record

Years from now, future generations need to know what it was actually like to be young right now. Not the sanitized version. The real one. That's what you're building here.
