This is information, not legal advice. SAFE's Letter Translator helps you understand what a letter is saying in plain English. For anything that could affect your housing, money, or legal status, talk to a qualified human: Citizens Advice (0800 144 8848), Shelter (0808 800 4444), StepChange for debt (0800 138 1111), or LawWorks for a free legal clinic. If you are under 18 and in crisis, call Childline (0800 1111). Letters are sent to our server, processed by Claude (Anthropic) to produce the translation, and then forgotten — we don't store, log, or train on what you paste.
