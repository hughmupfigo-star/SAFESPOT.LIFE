Emotional Regulation Fundamentals

A 4-module course on understanding your nervous system and learning how to regulate yourself.

Understanding your nervous system is the foundation everything else is built on.

Course Modules

(01)

Your Nervous System

What it is. How it works. Why it matters more than you think. The autonomic nervous system: sympathetic (fight/flight), parasympathetic (rest/digest), and the vagus nerve that holds it all together. How your past gets encoded in your nervous system. Why you can't think your way out of something your body learned.

(02)

Window of Tolerance & Polyvagal

Your window of tolerance: the optimal zone where you can process information and respond thoughtfully. What happens when you're outside it. The three states: social engagement, fight/flight, shutdown. How the vagus nerve connects it all. Why some people's windows are smaller. How to expand yours.

(03)

Baseline & Social Engagement

What your baseline is and why it matters. The social engagement system: how safety in your body enables connection with others. Why you can't be present in relationship when dysregulated. How early relational experiences shaped your baseline. How to gradually establish a more resourced baseline.

(04)

Recognising Dysregulation

The signs in your body. The sensations before thoughts arrive. How dysregulation announces itself. The pattern: trigger → nervous system response → behaviour → consequences. Why you often don't notice until you're already activated. Building awareness so you can intervene earlier.

(05)

How Dysregulation Shapes Life

The long-term impact of a chronically dysregulated nervous system. How it affects relationships, decision-making, health, work. Why you keep creating crises. The patterns that repeat. How dysregulation gets passed down. Why healing your nervous system changes everything.

(06)

Suppression & Physiology of Emotion

Why suppressing emotions doesn't make them disappear — it just stores them in your body. What happens physiologically when you push down feeling. How trauma gets trapped in tissue. The cost of chronic suppression. Why your body keeps the score. How emotion needs to move through you.

(07)

Processing & The Completion Cycle

What processing actually means. The complete cycle: feeling → acknowledgement → expression → integration. Why most people stop partway through. The difference between being overwhelmed and being present with emotion. How to stay in your window while processing. What completion feels like.

(08)

Your Regulation Toolkit

Practical, somatic tools you can use right now. Breathing techniques that shift your nervous system. Movement and shaking to discharge activation. Grounding and orienting practices. Cold water exposure. How to create safety in your body. Building your personal toolkit. How to know what you need when dysregulated.
