Why we host events

Community only happens when people show up.

Events are where SAFE becomes real. Not online. Not theoretical. Real people in real space, building real connection. Whether it's cleaning a park, playing chess, or eating dinner—every event is built on the same principle: showing up matters.

Our events are free or low-cost. They're inclusive. They're designed so that anybody, at any skill level, with any background, can show up and belong. Because that's what community actually looks like.
