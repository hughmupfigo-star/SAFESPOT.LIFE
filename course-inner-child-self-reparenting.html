The Inner Child & Self-Reparenting

A 4-module course on understanding the wounded part of you and learning how to care for it.

Why you do what you do — and the first practical steps toward changing it.

Course Modules

(01)

What Is the Inner Child

Understanding the part of you that formed before you could think your way around hurt. What the inner child carries. Why it still matters. How it shows up in every relationship, every decision, every moment you feel something you can't explain.

(02)

Developmental Windows & Strategies

How different stages of childhood development create different needs. What happens when those windows close without those needs being met. The strategies your inner child learned to survive. How those survival mechanisms now limit you.

(03)

Adaptations & Unmet Needs

The ways you adapted to environments that weren't safe. The fundamental needs that went unmet: safety, belonging, validation, seen-ness. How those unmet needs drive your adult choices. Why you keep recreating the same dynamic.

(04)

The Wound in the Body

Where trauma lives. Why talk therapy alone isn't enough. How your nervous system holds patterns. The somatic symptoms of an unhealed inner child. How to access what words can't reach.

(05)

Wounding & Identity

How childhood wounds become core beliefs about who you are. The identity you built to protect yourself. Why you keep proving these false beliefs are true. How to separate what happened to you from who you are.

(06)

The Reparenting Framework

What self-reparenting actually is. It's becoming the adult your inner child needed. The specific skills: protection, validation, permission, reassurance. How to give yourself what your caregivers couldn't. How to become your own secure base.

(07)

Reparenting in Practice

How to actually do this work. Exercises for talking to your inner child. Journal prompts that dig deep. How to notice when your inner child is activated. How to resource yourself in moments of dysregulation. Building rituals of care.

(08)

Building & Sustaining the Work

Making reparenting a practice, not a project. How to keep showing up for yourself. What happens when old patterns try to reassert. Building community with others doing this work. The long-term transformation that becomes possible.

About this course

This course is designed for anyone who recognises patterns in their life that don't make sense. For people who've felt abandoned. For those who've learned to make themselves smaller to be loved. For anyone who realises their adult decisions are being run by the part of them that's still eight years old.

You'll need nothing but an open mind. A journal helps. Time to reflect helps more.
