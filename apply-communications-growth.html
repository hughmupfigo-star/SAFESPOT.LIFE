Our Mission: No young person should apologise for existing.

SAFE is building holistic youth development hubs for at-risk young people. We're creating permanent foundations where young people can develop physically, creatively, strategically, emotionally, and civically. Every role in this organisation exists to serve that mission—to build a place where young people can show up fully as themselves, develop real skills, and find genuine community.

Communications & Growth Lead

You'll help more young people find SAFE. You'll develop our communications strategy, manage social channels, coordinate campaigns, and measure impact. You'll understand our audience deeply and figure out how to reach them where they are. You'll tell our story in ways that make people believe change is possible.

Location: UK (hybrid) · Type: Full-time

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop communications and growth strategy
  • Manage social media channels and content calendar
  • Coordinate PR campaigns and outreach
  • Create compelling narratives around SAFE's work
  • Track growth metrics and audience engagement
  • Collaborate with teams on messaging and positioning

You'll Learn

  • Growth marketing for social impact organisations
  • Community-first messaging and storytelling
  • How to reach at-risk young people authentically
  • Building narratives that shift perspectives
  • Measuring impact beyond vanity metrics
  • Cross-channel growth and audience building

We're Looking For

  • 3+ years in growth marketing, communications, or PR
  • Track record building engaged communities
  • Strong writing and storytelling skills
  • Experience with social media strategy and analytics
  • Understanding of young people and digital culture
  • Passion for authentic, values-driven messaging
