The line you didn't get.

What a good parent, carer, teacher or coach would have said in that moment. Write down a thing that happened — to you, today or a long time ago — and read the words you should have heard.

Not therapy — not stored — not judged.

Listening…
This is not therapy and it is not your relationship. It is the line that should have existed. Reading it might bring something up. If it does, talk to someone real: Samaritans 116 123 (free, 24/7), Childline 0800 1111 (under 19, free, confidential), or text SHOUT to 85258. For anything happening now that isn't safe, 999. Your text is sent to our server, used to write the lines, and immediately forgotten. We don't log it. We don't train on it. Anthropic's API doesn't train on it either.
