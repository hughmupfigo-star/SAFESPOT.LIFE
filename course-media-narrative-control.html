Course 07 · Truth Reversal Framework

Media &
Narrative Control

Immunity to Propaganda & Narrative Control. Narratives are weapons. Every story you consume is designed to shape what you believe, fear, want, and who you blame. This course teaches you how narratives work and how to spot the architecture before it shapes you.

What You'll Learn

  • Module 1: Narratives As Weapons — How stories are constructed to shape reality and why those who control narrative control everything else.
  • Module 2: The Architecture of Propaganda — The mechanics: repetition, emotion, tribal identity, enemy creation, and the lie told with such confidence it becomes truth.
  • Module 3: Media Literacy in the Attention Economy — How to read who benefits from the story, what's being hidden, and what you're being made to fear.
  • Module 4: The Hero & The Villain — Every narrative assigns roles. Understanding who the story wants you to be and who it wants you to hate.
  • Module 5: Algorithmic Narratives — How technology amplifies the narratives that divide you and profit from your engagement.
  • Module 6: Building Narrative Immunity — How to consume stories without letting them consume you. How to stay informed without being manipulated.

Immunity, Not Isolation

This isn't about avoiding media. It's about understanding how it works and building immunity to manipulation. You can stay informed about the world while resisting the narratives designed to weaponise your fear, anger, or shame.

The most powerful tool is a mind that sees how it's being shaped.
