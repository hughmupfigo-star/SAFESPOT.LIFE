Our Mission: No young person should apologise for existing.

SAFE is building holistic youth development hubs for at-risk young people. We're creating permanent foundations where young people can develop physically, creatively, strategically, emotionally, and civically. Every role in this organisation exists to serve that mission—to build a place where young people can show up fully as themselves, develop real skills, and find genuine community.

Community Manager

You'll be the connective tissue. You'll engage with people on our platform, moderate forums or chat channels, respond to enquiries, and build relationships. You'll understand what young people need to feel safe and supported online. You'll escalate issues to the right team. You'll make community feel like home.

Location: Remote · Type: Full-time

Key Responsibilities

  • Engage with community members and respond to enquiries
  • Moderate forums and chat channels for safety
  • Build relationships with regular members
  • Identify and escalate issues to appropriate teams
  • Create welcoming onboarding for new members
  • Gather feedback to improve community experience

You'll Learn

  • How to build safe online communities
  • Direct engagement with young people's needs
  • Conflict resolution and boundary-setting
  • Community health metrics and growth
  • Support protocols for vulnerable people
  • Creating belonging in digital spaces

We're Looking For

  • 2+ years managing online communities
  • Experience with moderation and safety
  • Strong communication and empathy skills
  • Ability to work with sensitive issues
  • Understanding of online safety and safeguarding
  • Commitment to building inclusive spaces
