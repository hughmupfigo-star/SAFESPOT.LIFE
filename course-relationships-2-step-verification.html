Relationships:
2-Step Verification

Identifying Red Flags in Partner Selection. Before you commit to someone, before you let them near your future—there are things you need to see. This course teaches you the patterns that predict dysfunction, the early warning signs, and the clarity needed to choose differently.

What You'll Learn

  • Module 1: The Patterns That Predict — What to look for in the first 3 months that tells you how the next 3 years will go.
  • Module 2: Love-Bombing & The Initial Mask — Why everyone looks good in the beginning and how to see through the performance.
  • Module 3: The Micro-Aggressions That Scale — Small cruelties that become the texture of the relationship.
  • Module 4: Dysfunction Archetypes — The controller, the abandoner, the provider who keeps score, the one who needs saving.
  • Module 5: Your Role in Dysfunction — Why you keep choosing the same pattern and what that reveals about your own wounding.
  • Module 6: The Exit — How to recognise when it's over and how to leave without destroying yourself.
  • Module 7: Choosing Differently — What to look for that actually predicts a functional partnership.

Why 2-Step Verification?

The first step is seeing the red flags. The second step is understanding why you ignored them. That's where the real work is—because until you understand why you chose to stay blind, you'll keep making the same choice with different people.

This course doesn't make you paranoid. It makes you clear.
